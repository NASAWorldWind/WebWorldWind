World Wind Shape Editor

Dragging the body of the shape moves the whole shape. Dragging a control point performs the action associated with that control point. The editor provides vertex insertion and removal for SurfacePolygon and SurfacePolyline. Single click when the cursor is over a shadow control point of a shape inserts a control point at that position. Double click when the cursor is over a control point removes that particular control point.

You can also manage the actions available for a particular shape by calling the edit method of ShapeEditor with the desired options. You can manage the move, reshape and rotate actions and you can also chose if you can add/remove control points on a shape (default values - all action are allowed). In our example we used the edit method in various ways:

  • Circle - you can do all the available actions (move, resize).
  • Ellipse - you can do resize (move and rotate are not allowed).
  • Placemark - you move it (this is the only action available).
  • Polygon - you move, rotate or manage the control points (you can't reshape it).
  • MultiPolygon - all the actions are available.
  • Polyline - all the actions are available.
  • Rectangle - you can move it (resize and rotate are not allowed).
  • Sector - all the actions are available.
